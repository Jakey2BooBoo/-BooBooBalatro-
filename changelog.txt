# BooBooBalatro
Balatro Mod featuring new Jokers, Enhancmenets, and Decks

## Changes (as of V0.1.1)
- Fixed blueprint compatibility labels
- Minor value/balance changes
- Minor text updates

## Features (as of V0.1.0)
- 17 new Jokers
  - 5 Commons based on the Love Languages
  - 7 Uncommons based on the Deadly Sins
  - 5 Rares based on the Stages of Grief
- 8 new Enhancements
  - 5 Upgrades to glass cards
  - 3 Upgrades to stone cards
- 2 Decks
  -Glass Joker
